### Issue Timeframe Resolution Service

#### Summary

The Issue Timeframe Resolution Service manages timeframes for resolving various issues within the system, based on the 'Issue Timeframe Resolution' table. It facilitates the setting, updating, and tracking of resolution timeframes for issues in different workspaces, ensuring efficient time management and adherence to deadlines.

#### Endpoints

1. **GetIssueTimeframesList**
    - Purpose: Retrieves a list of all issue timeframes.
    - Request: `GetIssueTimeframesListRequest`
    - Response: `GetIssueTimeframesListResponse`

2. **GetIssueTimeframe**
    - Purpose: Retrieves details of a specific issue timeframe.
    - Request: `GetIssueTimeframeRequest`
    - Response: `GetIssueTimeframeResponse`

3. **CreateIssueTimeframe**
    - Purpose: Creates a new issue timeframe.
    - Request: `CreateIssueTimeframeRequest`
    - Response: `CreateIssueTimeframeResponse`

4. **UpdateIssueTimeframe**
    - Purpose: Updates an existing issue timeframe.
    - Request: `UpdateIssueTimeframeRequest`
    - Response: `UpdateIssueTimeframeResponse`

5. **DeleteIssueTimeframe**
    - Purpose: Deletes an issue timeframe.
    - Request: `DeleteIssueTimeframeRequest`
    - Response: `DeleteIssueTimeframeResponse`
