### Template Management Service

#### Summary

The Template Management Service facilitates the handling of various templates within the application, such as for issues, reports, and communications. This service allows for the creation, retrieval, updating, and deletion of templates, aiding in standardizing and streamlining content creation.

#### Endpoints


1. **CreateTemplate**
    - **Purpose**: Create a new template for use within the system.
    - **Request Parameters**:
        - `title` (string): Title of the template.
        - `content` (string): Content or body of the template.
    - **Response**:
        - `template_id` (string): The ID of the newly created template.


2. **GetTemplate**
    - **Purpose**: Retrieve details of a specific template.
    - **Request Parameters**:
        - `template_id` (string): The ID of the template to retrieve.
    - **Response**:
        - `title` (string): The title of the template.
        - `content` (string): The content of the template.


3. **UpdateTemplate**
    - **Purpose**: Update an existing template's details.
    - **Request Parameters**:
        - `template_id` (string): The ID of the template to update.
        - `new_title` (string): The updated title of the template.
        - `new_content` (string): The updated content of the template.
    - **Response**:
        - `success` (Boolean): Indicates if the update was successful.


4. **DeleteTemplate**
    - **Purpose**: Delete a template from the system.
    - **Request Parameters**:
        - `template_id` (string): The ID of the template to be deleted.
    - **Response**:
        - `success` (Boolean): Indicates if the deletion was successful.
