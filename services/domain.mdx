### Domain Management Service

#### Summary

The Domain Management Service is responsible for managing the domain configurations in the 'Domains' table, particularly focusing on domains related to different workspaces. It provides functionalities for adding, retrieving, updating, and removing domain information, facilitating efficient domain management within the application.

#### Endpoints

1. **GetDomainsList**
    - Purpose: Retrieves a list of all domains.
    - Request: `GetDomainsListRequest`
    - Response: `GetDomainsListResponse`

2. **GetDomain**
    - Purpose: Retrieves details of a specific domain.
    - Request: `GetDomainRequest`
    - Response: `GetDomainResponse`

3. **CreateDomain**
    - Purpose: Creates a new domain.
    - Request: `CreateDomainRequest`
    - Response: `CreateDomainResponse`

4. **UpdateDomain**
    - Purpose: Updates an existing domain.
    - Request: `UpdateDomainRequest`
    - Response: `UpdateDomainResponse`

5. **DeleteDomain**
    - Purpose: Deletes a domain.
    - Request: `DeleteDomainRequest`
    - Response: `DeleteDomainResponse`
