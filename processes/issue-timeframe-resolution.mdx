## Overview

The "Issue Timeframe Resolution" system, in combination with the "Unified Filter" and "Workday Configuration", ensures efficient and timely management of issue resolution processes within the E-CMS. Teams or individuals, including department heads like the Division Chief, IELO Director, ODDGL, and the Director General (DG), can automatically set team specific resolution timeframe based on one or more rules, contributing to the overall 65-working-day limit.


1. Team-Specific Timeframes: Each team defines their specific timeframe for issue resolution, contributing to the aggregate 65-day resolution period.
2. Workdays Configuration and Adjustments: The system adjusts the resolution timeframe for non-working days (e.g., due to a typhoon) by adding extra time, preventing the issue status from incorrectly turning into ‘Breached’.
3. Re-Assignment Capability: Issues can be re-assigned within a team or move to another team closing the issue within their team and opening it on another team.
4. High-Risk Notification Alerts: When an issue enters the ‘High’ risk category (within 24 hours of the due date), notification alerts are sent to the concerned team or assignee.
